What We Do
More than a robotics team.
We are FIRST Tech Challenge.

FIRST Tech Challenge is a student-led program that goes far beyond building robots. We are engineers, programmers, designers, marketers, fundraisers, and community leaders — all on the same team. FTC develops the skills that colleges and employers actually look for, wrapped in one of the most competitive student programs in the world.

⚙ Engineering & Design

Students design, build, and iterate a competition robot from scratch each season — learning mechanical engineering, CAD concepts, and hands-on fabrication in a real design cycle.

💻 Programming

We code autonomous robot behavior and driver-control systems using Java. Members develop real software engineering skills — the same language used in professional Android development.

📋 Business & Marketing

Every team manages a brand, pitches to sponsors, writes a business plan, and markets itself to judges — building skills in communication, finance, and entrepreneurship.

🌟 Community Outreach

FTC teams earn awards for giving back. We run STEM events, mentor younger students, and connect with our community — outreach is scored just as much as robot performance.

🏆 Competition

Each season a new game challenge is released. Teams compete in qualifying tournaments, state championships, and the FIRST World Championship in Houston — against thousands of teams globally.

🎓 Scholarships

FIRST Tech Challenge alumni have access to tens of millions of dollars in college scholarships. 61% of FIRST alumni declare a major in engineering or computer science by their fourth year of college.
